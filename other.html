The Perseus Project, at Tufts University
Perseus is an evolving digital library focusing on the ancient Greek world. It is a companion site to a multimedia CD-ROM. The Perseus Project Web site provides Greek and Latin texts, many also in English translation.

The Online Medieval and Classical Library
The Online Library is a collection of important classical and medieval works, searchable and organized by title, author, genre, and language.

Project Gutenberg master index
The index is an unofficial list of the many Project Gutenberg electronic texts.

The Electronic Text Center, at the University of Virginia
The center offers a number of searchable public collections, including a large modern English collection.

The Christian Classics Ethereal Library
The library provides classic Christian books in electronic format.
